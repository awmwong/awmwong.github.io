The Working Group

Mobile Application Developer

The Working Group (TWG) is a web and mobile development shop based in Toronto. They design, build, and maintain applications for an ever growing list of clients.

I interned at TWG for 4 months between May 2012 and August 2012. While there, I was involved in several different projects, which included leading the development of the Toronto International Film Festival app for Blackberry. I also worked on the 1THING iOS app, where I was responsible for maintenance and developing new features.
